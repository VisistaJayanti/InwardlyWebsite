Our Mission

  • o To acknowledge the challenges individuals face in openly addressing their mental health concerns within peer and workplace environments, often due to the fear of criticism and societal stigma.
  • o To establish a confidential platform that offers a secure space for individuals of all age groups to engage in candid discussions with professional doctors and experts.
  • o To destigmatizing mental health issues, fostering an environment where individuals feel comfortable seeking support without the fear of judgment.
  • o We aim to promote open dialogue, understanding, and professional assistance for those navigating mental health concerns.

Our Vision

The vision of the mental health startup Inwardly is to create a safe and supportive platform where individuals with mental disorders can openly discuss their difficulties without fear or hesitation. Inwardly aims to break down the stigma surrounding mental health by providing a judgment-free space for people to share their experiences, seek support, and connect with others facing similar challenges. By offering a range of resources, such as online support groups, counseling services, and educational materials, Inwardly strives to empower individuals to take control of their mental health and seek the help they need. The ultimate goal of Inwardly is to improve the overall well-being of its users and contribute to a more compassionate and understanding society when it comes to mental health issues.
