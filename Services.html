Mental Health Information

Anxiety Disorders

Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD) is a common mental health condition characterized by excessive and persistent worry about everyday things. People with GAD may feel anxious most days and find it difficult to control their worry, even when there is little or no reason for concern.

Sleeping Disorders

Sleep disorders are conditions that affect the quality, amount and timing of sleep you're able to get at night. Common sleep disorders include insomnia, restless legs syndrome, narcolepsy and sleep apnea. Sleep disorders can affect your mental health and physical health.
